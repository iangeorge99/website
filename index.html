Success Keys Youth Services (SKYS)

SKYS provides day reporting and family support services for youth on juvenile probation in Nebraska. We help youth build routines, accountability, and practical skills—while keeping families and supervising teams aligned through clear expectations and communication.

  • Family Support Services: in-home coaching focused on routines, communication, and follow-through

Programs

Two core services

DS

Day SKYS (Day Reporting)

Structured weekday programming that supports supervision, academic monitoring, life skills development, and positive routines. Includes supervised community-based activities such as YMCA visits, library study sessions, and structured recreation.

See Day Reporting details →
FS

Family Support Services

In-home and community-based coaching focused on communication, routines, boundaries, and consistent follow-through. Services are coordinated with supervising teams and aligned with goal planning.

How it works

Clear steps from referral to services

1

Referral

Reach out by email or phone with supervision expectations, school plan, and key goals.

2

Intake

We meet with the youth and caregiver and coordinate expectations with the supervising team.

3

Start services

We begin programming and provide updates aligned with the supervising team’s needs.

Transportation

SKYS provides transportation to and from the center within a 30-mile radius, coordinated during referral and intake.
