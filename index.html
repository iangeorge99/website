What we do

Success Keys Youth Services currently offers two core program areas designed to support youth growth, accountability, and stability through community-based structure and support.

Day SKYS – Day Reporting Program

A non-treatment, community-based day reporting program providing structured supervision, academic support, life skills development, and supervised prosocial engagement for youth involved with juvenile probation.

  • Academic monitoring and daily schoolwork blocks
  • Life skills practice and goal planning
  • Structured supervision and accountability
  • Supervised community-based activities

Family Support Services

Community-based family support focused on guidance, coordination, and skill development to strengthen routines, communication, and positive outcomes in the home.

  • Support with routines, structure, and follow-through
  • Communication and problem-solving support
  • Goal-focused guidance for youth and caregivers
  • Coordination and check-ins as needed

Program highlights

  • Mentorship and supportive guidance
  • Goal-setting and progress check-ins
  • Skills practice: communication, routines, and decision-making
  • Positive use of free time and healthy activities

Who we serve

  • Youth seeking additional support and structure
  • Families looking for community-based guidance
  • Referrals from community partners (when applicable)
